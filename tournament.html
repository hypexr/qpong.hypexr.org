Qpong Tournament has quietly passed
Unfortunately the qpong tournament didn't work out very well for several reasons.  I would like to see a QPong league form where QPongers can meet at scheduled times and get a few games in.  If anyone is interested in helping to form this league e-mail me at qpong@qp.org.

Its time for the tournament begin!
Here is how its going to work:  Each team will play every other team who is registered for the tournament. When all matches are played the wins for each team will be counted.  If ties occur, in the number of wins, there will be play off games between the teams who have tied.  Bellow is a calendar where teams will sign up for ALL of the days and times when their team is available to play matches.  When two teams are free for a match at the same time then that time slot will become theirs to play on the Kannibas Match Server.  Information about all matches must be sent to qpong@qp.org prior to the match in order for it to be official.  Teams are allowed to agree on a date and time to play the match but qpong@qp.org must be notified so a ref. can be assigned to the match. 

Here is the Calendar!  Please send me the dates and times when your team can play and your team will be added.

Rules:
1. Each team must consist of 3 players and may have additional subs.
2. All players from each team must be present for the battle to begin.
3. There will be a ref for every match (group of battles) that is played.  This ref must be present for the match to begin.  Persons will either volunteer to be a ref or persons may be requested to ref a match.
4. Every battle must have a ref present to watch over the game, change the maps, and make sure that none of the players have frozen.  If a player is frozen or has left the game then the ref will tell everyone to halt the game.  If a ref halts the game then all players must stop shooting and moving.  The ref will let the game continue when all players are present again.
5. To determine which team is the winner, three games will be played each on a different map (these three maps will be the result of a vote from everyone involved in the tournament). 
6. Screen shots of the final score must be taken by the ref but all players are encouraged to take a screen shot incase the ref doesn't and to avoid conflicting stories. :)
